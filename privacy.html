Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

At Tether Tags, protecting your privacy is at the core of our service. We are committed to ensuring that your personal information remains secure while providing you with a reliable asset recovery experience.

Information We Collect

We collect only the essential information needed to keep you connected:

  • Contact Information: Your phone number, used exclusively to route WhatsApp notifications when your tag is scanned.

Data Usage & Privacy

Your contact details are used solely for the purpose of notifying you of your tag activity. We act as a secure bridge—the person scanning your tag never sees your actual phone number. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party marketers.

Your Security

We are committed to maintaining the security of your data. We do not require you to create an account or download any applications, ensuring zero friction and maximum privacy.

Your Rights

You maintain control over your data. You may contact us at any time to request the deletion of your account or the modification of your contact information.
